SPICY CARDS ONLINE

How to Play

Spicy Cards Online is an exciting game designed to spice up your relationship. Choose a game mode and let the fun begin!

🌶️ Him & Her

  • Each player will generate a card from each category you like (or them all).
  • Follow the instructions on the card.
  • Continue playing until you decide to stop or complete all challenges!

💏 Couples

  • Each player will take turns on generating a card
  • Follow the instructions on the card
  • Complete the card before the next player's turn.
  • Play for as many rounds as you like!
